The inventory application streamlines the collection and labeling of image training data.

The end goal is to allow a user to predict 🪄 a product label by uploading an image of the product.


To add training data, follow the steps below:

Step 1. Take a photo of the inventory iteration using a smart phone. 📲

Step 2. Select the data label from the Inventory Classification section that matches the inventory product. 🏷️
